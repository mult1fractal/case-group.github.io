    Welcome to the CaSe Group webpage

    The CaSe Group (led by Dr. Christian Brandt) is a young and dynamic team at the Institute for Infectious Diseases and Infection Control.

    We combine the wet-lab biology (especially nanopore sequencing) with applied bioinformatics (workflow development via cloud) to address various clinical needs.
